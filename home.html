Endless Platformer

Jump, collect, and survive in this thrilling endless adventure!

How to Play

  • Arrow Keys: Move left and right
  • Up Arrow: Jump
  • Goal: Collect stars and avoid bombs
  • Time Limit: 60 seconds to score as high as possible

Features

  • Endless vertical platforming
  • Moving platforms for extra challenge
  • Dynamic bomb AI that patrols platforms
  • Smooth animations and physics
  • Height tracking system

Tip: Collect stars to increase your score, but watch out for the bombs!
